Welcome to my web pages! I am PhD Candidate student in Natural Language Processing and Research Engineer at the Information Processing Laboratory, that is part of the Department of Informatics of Athens University of Economics and Business, under the supervision of Prof. Ion Androutsopoulos and a fresh graduate from the MSc in Machine Learning at KTH Royal Institute of Technology.

About me

My main motivation in studying Deep Learning lies not only in the field’s promising application potentials but also in its underlying technical and scientific depth. I am currently a PhD student at Athens University of Economics and Business, focusing on multi-step textual entailment, as well as a member of the AUEB's NLP Group. I recently completed the MSc in Machine Learning at KTH Royal Institute of Technology, the most prestigeous technical university in Sweden, known for its continuing excellence in computer science and the fact that teaching is informed by research towards the development of advanced machine learning systems. During my studies at KTH, where I was involved in the research carried out by NeuralDynamicsLab at SciLife, I have also received Best Performance Award in Spring Semester 2020-21; Karl Engvers' Award for my excellent performance in Deep Learning in Data Science, Project course in Individual Biology, as well as Speech and Language Processing in 2021. I published work in ReScience Journal and CLEF working notes in 2022 on behalf of the lab's participation in ImageCLEF medical 2022. I developed dense retrieval methods for biomedical documents, text generation pipelines, convolutional image encoders for diagnostic captioning and combinations of all the above. Furthermore, I recently conducted a six-month internship at Alpha Bank Optimization Group as a Machine Learning Engineer, to contribute to the bank's Alpha Blueprint Reform. I researched novel Deep Learning approaches related to Natural Language Processing and use them to develop element extraction and text generation architectures in Greek businesses corpora and explored potential improvements of Microsoft's FAST Search Algorithm for document and snippet retrieval. You can find more information about me in my LinkedIn profile and my Curriculum Vitae. My publications are available on-line. My web pages also contain information on the research I have conducted, and ways to contact me.
